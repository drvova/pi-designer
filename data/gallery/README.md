# Golden gallery

Three complete pages produced by this system that meet its bar. They are
calibration references, not templates: open the one whose aesthetic family is
closest to your design read, study how it earns its choices, then build
something that would look wrong next to it.

| Example | Family | What it demonstrates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `kinematica/` | industrial/utilitarian, light | live controller-panel anchor with DEMO badge, spec table, product-specific headline voice, restraint with one warm accent |
| `plane/` | editorial/magazine | masthead with dateline, drop caps, two-column body, kickers and standfirsts, pull quote with rules, print colophon |
| `leak/` | handcraft/warm | brand concept built from the name, figurative furniture line drawings, oversized ampersand transition, joint diagram, wood-grain texture swatch, dimension-specced pieces |

Do not copy their palettes, faces, or copy verbatim — the point of each is
that it could only belong to its own product. Every page here: content
legible at initial paint, no hotlinked assets, demo data labelled, colophon
in the footer.
