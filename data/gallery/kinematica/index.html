PHILOSOPHY

Every micron is a decision.

Robotic precision is not a spec sheet number — it is the sum of mechanical stiffness, thermal stability, encoder resolution, and control-loop latency. The KT-7 was designed from the spindle up for SMT and semiconductor applications where a 0.1° temperature shift means a rejected component.

Cast from aged cast iron for vibration damping. Direct-drive joints with zero backlash. A kinematic model compensates for thermal expansion across the operating range. The result is an arm that holds position through a full production shift without re-homing.
