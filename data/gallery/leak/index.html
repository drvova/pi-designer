How we work

Four stages, no shortcuts. Every piece is made start to finish in our workshop.

01

Draw

We start with drawings at full scale. Proportions, joinery locations, grain direction — every detail decided before the first cut.

02

Choose

Timber is selected board by board. We pair grain patterns, check moisture content, and let the wood acclimatise in the studio before cutting.

03

Make

Hand-cut joinery, hand-planed surfaces. Machines assist with dimensioning; every visible surface is finished by hand with plane and scraper.

04

Finish

Natural oil and wax. Three coats, hand-rubbed, left to cure for a week. The piece goes out when the finish has settled — not before.

Mortise and tenon — the joint that holds every piece together.
Cut by hand, fitted by eye, fastened with a wedge.

The studio

We work from a small workshop on the edge of town. Natural light, hand tools within reach, a record player in the corner. Commissions are welcome — every piece starts with a conversation about what you need and the wood that suits it.
