How to use this guide
This guide is not about replacing designers with AI — it’s about learning to collaborate with it effectively.
To get the most value, use the 4D Framework by Anthropic as your mindset for working with AI

Delegation

Know what to delegate. Be clear about your goal and decide which parts
of the work are worth automating. AI is great at analysis, structure,
and synthesis — but meaning, taste, and direction stay yours.

Description

Explain what needs to be done. Good output starts with a good prompt.
Describe the context, goal, and constraints clearly. Add examples
and criteria for success — clarity is creativity here.

Discernment

Evaluate the result critically. AI can generate fast, but only you can judge
quality. Compare the outcome to your standards — consistency,
accessibility, brand fit. Be the editor, not just the receiver.

Diligence

Stay responsible for the final outcome. AI supports you, but you own
the result. Design decisions, documentation, and quality assurance
remain your craft.
